Reading Resources

  • Hogg, Robert V.; McKean, Joseph; and Craig, Allen T., "Introduction to Mathematical Statistics (6th Edition)" (2005)
  • Casella, G., & Berger, R. (2024). Statistical Inference (2nd ed.). Chapman and Hall/CRC. https://doi.org/10.1201/9781003456285
  • Vaart AW van der. Asymptotic Statistics. Cambridge University Press; 1998.

Material

Week 1: Introduction to statistical inference; reminder on random variables and distributions, statistical model, examples of models, likelihood function.

Week 2: Definition of likelihood; maximum likelihood estimator; examples on maximum likelihood estimators; definition of expectation and moments, method of moments; example on method of moments estimation.
